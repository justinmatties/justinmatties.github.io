Route66 Research App

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

Scope

The Route66 Research App is a research data collection tool intended for enrolled study participants. It is not a public consumer service.

Information handled by the app

Depending on the study procedures, the app may process information entered by participants, camera recordings created during study tasks, activity-related records, notification settings, and technical information needed to operate the app and synchronize research files with Google Drive.

How information is used

Information collected through the app is used to conduct research procedures, monitor study completion, troubleshoot app access or operation, and manage research records. It is not used for advertising or promotional purposes.

Storage and access

Research data may be synchronized to research-managed Google Drive storage and accessed by authorized research personnel. Data handling, retention, and any sharing are governed by the study consent materials, approved research procedures, and applicable institutional requirements.

Participant questions and requests

Participation details and research data rights are described in the applicable study consent materials. For privacy questions or requests concerning research data, contact:
