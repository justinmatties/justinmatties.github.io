About Me

I am Justin Matties, a biomechanics researcher and PhD student at UMass Amherst. My work focuses on understanding the biomechanics of running, exploring sex-specific responses to advanced footwear, injury interventions, and personalized equipment design.

With expertise in biomechanics, data science, and computational statistics, I integrate advanced tools to analyze gait mechanics and their effects on performance, pain reduction, and clinical outcomes.
