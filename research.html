Research

Master's Research

My master's research investigated the biomechanical and physiological effects of advanced footwear technology ("super shoes") on distance running performance and training adaptations. This research analyzed sex-specific responses to carbon-plated shoes and their impact on running economy, biomechanics, and physiological adaptations.

Current Projects

  • Exploring sex-specific biomechanical responses to variable-pressure footwear and injury interventions.
  • Using computational tools for individualized gait analysis and injury prevention strategies.
  • Integrating wearable technology to monitor biomechanics and physiological adaptations.
